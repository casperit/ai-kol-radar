The weekend AI conversation stays hot, organized around four converging threads. **Agents going to production** is a daily drumbeat — teams are publishing real results from multi-agent pipelines across code review, research reports, and customer support. **Vibe coding** enters a new phase as AI-native IDEs like Cursor and Windsurf ship new versions, and agentic coding tools such as Claude Code and Codex dominate developer discussion. **Multimodal creation** matures further: next-gen image and video models are being dissected into repeatable creative workflows, and solo creators shipping AI-made content is now the baseline playbook.
